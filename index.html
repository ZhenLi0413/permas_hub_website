PERMAS Hub

Connect, Organize, and Engage with PERMAS.

PERMAS Hub is a centralized mobile platform for PERMAS UTM that helps manage events, announcements, feedback, member applications, profiles, and reports in one place.

The Problem

PERMAS coordination needs one reliable center.

01

PERMAS information and event updates are scattered across multiple platforms.

02

Students may miss important event announcements.

03

Committee members face difficulty managing member applications, event participation, feedback, and reports manually.

The Solution

One integrated mobile application for members and committee teams.

PERMAS Hub provides one integrated mobile application where members can view announcements, register for events, submit feedback, and manage their profiles. Committee members can manage applications, track participation, and view reports and analytics.

Key Features

Built for the way PERMAS works.

E

Event Management

Create, update, and organize PERMAS events from one structured system.

R

Event Registration

Allow members to register for activities and keep participation records organized.

N

Announcements & Notifications

Share important updates so students can follow PERMAS news without searching across platforms.

F

Feedback System

Collect member feedback to support better events, communication, and services.

P

Profile Management

Help members maintain their personal information and PERMAS-related records.

A

D

Reports & Analytics

View summaries and analytics that make event participation and member activity easier to understand.
